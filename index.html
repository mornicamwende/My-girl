Welcome to my girl international

we recognise that girls and women in the world face social, cultural, institutional and systemic barriers to their rights. These include child marriage, poverty, child labour, female genital cutting/mutilation, gender-based violence and lack of information on sexual reproductive health and rights. As a rights-based organization, we remain committed to working with the hard to reach and marginalized girls and women in communities to enhance their quality of life. We partner with National Governments, development partners, public and private partners, membership organizations and other like-minded organizations and communities to realize the Global Goals related to girls and women. Our programs target vulnerable and marginalized girls and women for inclusive development, quality education, good health and sustainable livelihoods. We advocate for inclusive laws and policies protecting girls and women ssssto enhance their participation.
